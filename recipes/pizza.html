Thin-Crust Pizza Dough Recipe

Fast and easy to make thin crust pizza

Quick 15 minute crispy pizza easy to learn and just as easy to make

Ingredients

  • flour
  • yeast
  • Sugar and water
  • salt

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast
  2. Make the dough
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Roll the dough into a circle
